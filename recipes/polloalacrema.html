Pollo A La Crema

Delicious creamy pollo a la crema, real basic like

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • a half pound of button or crimini mushrooms (they're the same thing)
  • a half stick of butter
  • a pound of chimken meat
  • A spoon of cumin
  • A spoon of smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • a few cloves of garlic
  • a pint of heavy cream
  • Optionally some melty cheese of some kind
  1. Slice that onion real thin
  2. Slice up them mushrooms
  3. Cut up the chicken meat into bite sized pieces
  4. Crush, or finely chop the garlic
  5. Heat up the pan, throw the butter in there
  6. When the butter is melted, throw in the onions
  7. When the onions are halfway to what you consider done, throw in the mushrooms
  8. Toss in the chicken
  9. Throw in your seasonings
  10. All of it should be done cooking, once it's done, pour in like a half pint of heavy cream
  11. let it all simmer until it thickens up
  12. You're done. Serve this over rice, or next to beans and rice
