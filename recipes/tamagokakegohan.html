Tamago Kake Gohan

A light, hearty breakfast. Quick, easy, leaves you satisfied

Ingredients

  • A bowl of steaming hot rice
  • An egg
  • Furikake of some flavor
  • Soy sauce
  • Optionals:
    • Crispy fried onion, garlic, jalapenos, etc.
    • Chili oil
    • Chopped breakfast meats (bacon, sausage, etc)
  1. Take your steaming hot rice and put it into a bowl
    • If your rice isn't hot enough, you're going to have a bad time
  3. Break the egg into the hot rice
  4. Stir, and slowly whipe the egg into the rice until the egg no longer leaves behind strings of white, and the egg coats all the rice
  5. Add soy sauce to your preference and mix it evenly into the rice
  6. Throw in the rest of your optionals
  7. Enjoy breakfast
