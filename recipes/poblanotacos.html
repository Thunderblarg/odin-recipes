Poblano Tacos

They're so easy, and sooo good

Ingredients

  • 4 big poblano peppers
  • 1 cup of meltable cheese (oaxaca preferred, but whatever floats your boat)
  • Honestly this can be it, you slap it on tortillas and just eat it as is, but you can add a lot more to this
    • Chopped up breakfast meat (bacon, sausage, etc)
    • Chopped onions
    • Mushrooms
    • Some kind of salsa (Salsa verde, pico de gallo)
    • Sliced scallions
    • Definitely guacamole
  1. Deseed the poblanos and chop them into like inch sized bits
  2. Heat up a pan, get it real hot
  3. The poblanos take about ten minutes to cook, so anything else on the stove has to line up with that
  4. The poblanos should have a light char to them when they're done
  5. Melt some cheese over everything once they're done, and let the cheese get a nice crust
  6. Heat up some tortillas and scoop some of the good stuff on them, mmmm mm yum yum
